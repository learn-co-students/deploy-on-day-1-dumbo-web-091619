Biography

I am a digital artist from New York and graduated from Parsons The New School For Design. For four years, my main focus was Animation, Photoshop, 3D Modeling, and Character Drawing that would allow me to create designs on my own. In addition to 3D digital design, I also have a deep passion for photography, 3D Printing, computer technician, & origami.
